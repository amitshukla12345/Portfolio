About Me

Hello! My name is Amit Shukla. I am a passionate and dedicated Java Full stack Developer with a strong interest in building scalable, responsive, and user-friendly web applications. As a fresher, I bring enthusiasm, curiosity, and a solid foundation in both frontend and backend technologies.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications (BCA) from Tilak Maharashtra Vidyapeeth, Pune, and I’m currently completing a Full Stack Java Development course at IT Vedant. I’ve also earned an IBM certification in Java to strengthen my backend skills.

My technical skill set includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, Java, Spring Boot, MySQL, and beginner-level knowledge of React. I have developed projects like a Notes Sharing Website, a Save Nature awareness site, and a Railway Booking System, which helped me understand how to apply programming concepts in real-world scenarios.

I am highly motivated to join a dynamic team where I can grow as a developer, contribute to meaningful projects, and continuously improve through hands-on experience and mentorship. My ultimate goal is to become a well-rounded full-stack developer and build solutions that improve everyday life.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, reading about software trends, and solving logical puzzles. I’m a quick learner, a good listener, and someone who thrives in a collaborative environment.
